Description

Pizza is an Italian dish which typically consists of cheese layered over tomato sauce all on a round formation of dough. There are many variations of pizza and it can have many toppings and different types of sauces. Some popular toppings are mushrooms, peppers, onions, and olives. The one above is a simple cheese pizza with no extra toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Spread the pizza dough into a circle shape of your preferred size.
  2. Put a few tablespoons of tomato sauce on the dough and spread it evenly across the surface.
  3. If the mozzarella cheese isn't shredded, grate it using a cheese grater. Sprinkle the mozzarella evenly over the sauce.
  4. Bake for 18-25 minutes at 400° F, or 205° C. Let it cool for 5 minutes and enjoy!
